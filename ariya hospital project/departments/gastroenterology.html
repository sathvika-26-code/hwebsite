About Our Gastroenterology Department

The Department of Gastroenterology at Ariya Hospitals provides comprehensive care for disorders affecting the digestive system, including the esophagus, stomach, intestines, liver, pancreas, and gallbladder. Our team of experienced specialists is dedicated to providing personalized care using the latest diagnostic and therapeutic approaches.

From routine screenings to treatment of complex gastrointestinal conditions, we offer a full spectrum of services focused on preventive care, accurate diagnosis, and effective treatment plans. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure that patients receive the highest standard of care in a comfortable and supportive environment.

When to Consult a Gastroenterologist

Gastroenterologists specialize in diagnosing and treating various disorders of the digestive system. Beyond routine screenings like colonoscopies, our specialists are here to help with specific concerns ranging from chronic digestive discomfort to liver diseases and inflammatory bowel conditions.

GERD/Acid Reflux IBS Crohn's Disease Ulcerative Colitis Liver Disease Gallstones Pancreatitis Colon Polyps Celiac Disease Digestive Cancers

Our Approach

At Ariya Hospitals, we take a holistic approach to digestive health. We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients based on trust and open communication. Our team works collaboratively to develop individualized treatment plans that address each patient's unique digestive health needs and concerns.

Our Treatments & Services

  • Diagnostic Endoscopy

    Advanced upper and lower endoscopic procedures to visualize and diagnose conditions affecting the digestive tract, including gastroscopy, colonoscopy, and capsule endoscopy.

  • Therapeutic Endoscopy

    Minimally invasive endoscopic procedures for treatment of various conditions, including polyp removal, stricture dilation, stent placement, and endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography (ERCP).

  • Hepatology Services

    Specialized care for liver diseases including hepatitis, cirrhosis, fatty liver disease, and comprehensive liver function assessments.

  • Inflammatory Bowel Disease Management

    Personalized treatment approaches for Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis, including the latest biological therapies and immunomodulators.

  • Functional GI Disorder Treatment

    Comprehensive management of functional disorders like irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), functional dyspepsia, and chronic constipation.

  • Colorectal Cancer Screening

    Advanced screening protocols for early detection of colorectal cancers and precancerous polyps, following international best practices.
