About Our General Medicine Department

The Department of General Medicine at Ariya Hospitals serves as the foundation of our healthcare services, providing comprehensive care for a wide range of medical conditions. Our general physicians are your first point of contact for health concerns, offering expert diagnosis, treatment, and management of both acute and chronic conditions.

Our approach combines thorough clinical assessment with preventive care strategies to maintain and improve your overall health. With a focus on building long-term doctor-patient relationships, we ensure personalized care that addresses your unique health needs and concerns.

When to Consult a General Physician

A general physician is trained to diagnose and treat a broad spectrum of health conditions. From seasonal illnesses to chronic disease management, our general physicians provide comprehensive care for patients of all ages. If you're experiencing unexplained symptoms, need preventive healthcare advice, or require management of ongoing health conditions, our general medicine specialists are here to help.

Common Cold & Flu Hypertension Diabetes Respiratory Infections Allergies Digestive Issues Thyroid Disorders Fever Headaches Joint Pain

Our Approach

At Ariya Hospitals, our general physicians take a holistic approach to healthcare. We believe in treating not just the symptoms but addressing the underlying causes of illness. Our team works collaboratively with patients to develop individualized treatment plans that consider their medical history, lifestyle factors, and personal health goals.

Our Services

  • Diagnosis and Treatment of Common Illnesses

    Comprehensive care for seasonal illnesses, infections, and common health concerns with effective treatment plans tailored to your specific needs.

  • Chronic Disease Management

    Specialized care for long-term conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and thyroid disorders with regular monitoring and medication management.

  • Preventive Health Check-ups

    Comprehensive health screenings and assessments to detect potential health issues early and provide preventive interventions.

  • Lifestyle and Nutrition Counseling

    Expert advice on maintaining a healthy lifestyle, including dietary recommendations, exercise plans, and stress management techniques.

  • Immunizations and Vaccinations

    Up-to-date vaccination services for all age groups following international immunization guidelines.

  • Health Risk Assessments

    Comprehensive evaluation of personal health risks based on family history, lifestyle factors, and medical conditions.
