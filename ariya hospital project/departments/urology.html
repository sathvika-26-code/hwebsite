About Our Urology Department

The Department of Urology at Ariya Hospitals specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting the urinary tract in men and women, as well as disorders of the male reproductive system. Our expert team of urologists combines extensive clinical experience with advanced technology to provide comprehensive care for patients of all ages.

We offer both surgical and non-surgical treatment options for urological conditions, with a focus on minimally invasive techniques whenever possible. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated staff ensure that patients receive the highest quality of care in a supportive and comfortable environment.

When to Consult a Urologist

If you're experiencing issues with your urinary system or male reproductive organs, our specialists can provide expert diagnosis and treatment. Our urologists treat a wide range of conditions affecting the kidneys, ureters, bladder, urethra, and male reproductive organs.

Kidney Stones Urinary Tract Infections BPH Prostate Cancer Urinary Incontinence Erectile Dysfunction Male Infertility Bladder Cancer Kidney Cancer Urethral Strictures

Our Approach

At Ariya Hospitals, our urologists take a patient-centered approach to diagnosis and treatment. We understand that urological conditions can be sensitive and often affect quality of life. Our team prioritizes patient comfort, education, and involvement in decision-making while providing the most effective treatments available.

Our Treatments & Services

  • Comprehensive Urological Evaluation

    Complete diagnostic services including urinalysis, imaging studies, cystoscopy, and urodynamic testing to accurately diagnose urological conditions.

  • Minimally Invasive Surgery

    Advanced laparoscopic and robotic-assisted procedures for kidney, prostate, and bladder conditions, offering reduced recovery time and minimal scarring.

  • Stone Disease Management

    Comprehensive treatment options for kidney stones including shock wave lithotripsy (SWL), ureteroscopy, percutaneous nephrolithotomy (PCNL), and preventive metabolic evaluation.

  • Prostate Disease Treatment

    Specialized care for benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) and prostate cancer, including transurethral resection of the prostate (TURP), laser procedures, and advanced cancer therapies.

  • Urinary Incontinence Solutions

    Personalized treatments for male and female urinary incontinence, including medical management, pelvic floor therapy, and surgical interventions when necessary.

  • Men's Sexual Health Services

    Comprehensive evaluation and treatment for erectile dysfunction, premature ejaculation, low testosterone, and other men's health concerns.
