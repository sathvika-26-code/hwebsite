About Our Radiology Department

The Department of Radiology and Imaging at Ariya Hospitals boasts of using state-of-the-art and the latest equipment, providing expert diagnostic and interventional services to support the clinical needs of our hospital. Our team of skilled radiologists and technicians utilizes advanced imaging technology to detect, diagnose, and treat various medical conditions.

Radiology, also known as diagnostic imaging, is a group of tests that take pictures or images of various body parts. Our department is committed to delivering precise diagnostic results with minimal waiting times, ensuring the highest standards of patient care and safety throughout the imaging process.

Our Approach to Radiology

The field of radiology is divided into two branches: diagnostic radiology and interventional radiology, both of which use radiant energy to diagnose and treat diseases. Our expert radiologists examine the results of imaging tests to identify relevant findings that evaluate and support accurate diagnoses.

Following the completion of imaging tests, our radiologists provide comprehensive reports with their interpretations to the referring clinical doctors, ensuring a collaborative approach to patient care. We prioritize patient comfort and safety while delivering high-quality diagnostic services.

Applications of Radiology

Radiology is used in every aspect of healthcare, supporting various medical specialties and treatments. Our department provides crucial imaging support for:

Surgery Pediatrics Obstetrics Oncology Trauma-response Emergency Medicine Infectious Disease Cardiology Neurology Orthopedics

Our Services & Procedures

  • X-Ray Imaging

    Comprehensive X-ray services including plain X-rays, bedside X-rays, tomography, and fluoroscopy for diagnosing bone fractures, joint conditions, chest problems, and more.

  • CT Scanning

    Advanced computed tomography (CT) scans that provide detailed cross-sectional images of organs, bones, and tissues for diagnosing injuries, tumors, and other conditions.

  • MRI Services

    Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) that uses strong magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of the body's internal structures, particularly effective for brain, spine, joint, and soft tissue imaging.

  • Ultrasound & Doppler Studies

    Comprehensive ultrasound services including obstetric scans, color Doppler studies, neonatal cranial USG, fetal anomaly USG, TRUS & TVS, and high-resolution ultrasound for musculoskeletal and small parts.

  • Interventional Radiology

    Minimally invasive procedures using USG and fluoroscopy guidance, allowing for diagnosis and treatment with reduced risk, less pain, and shorter recovery times compared to traditional surgery.

  • Pediatric Imaging

    Specialized imaging procedures for children with equipment and protocols designed specifically for pediatric patients, ensuring minimal radiation exposure and maximum comfort.
