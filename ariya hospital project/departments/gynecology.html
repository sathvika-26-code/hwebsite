About Our Gynecology Department

The Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Ariya Hospitals provides comprehensive medical treatment for gynecological issues as well as complete obstetric services. Our team of experienced specialists is dedicated to providing personalized care for women at every stage of life.

From adolescence through menopause and beyond, we offer a full spectrum of services focused on preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment of conditions affecting women's reproductive health. Our state-of-the-art facilities ensure that patients receive the highest standard of care in a comfortable and supportive environment.

When to Consult a Gynecologist

Regular gynecological check-ups are essential for maintaining optimal women's health. Beyond routine visits, our specialists are here to help with specific concerns ranging from reproductive health issues to pregnancy care and menopausal symptoms.

Our Approach

At Ariya Hospitals, we take a holistic approach to women's health. We believe in building lasting relationships with our patients based on trust and open communication. Our team works collaboratively to develop individualized treatment plans that address each woman's unique health needs and concerns.

Our Treatments & Services

  • Comprehensive Gynecological Care

    Complete range of services including annual check-ups, PAP smears, HPV vaccination, and treatment for various gynecological conditions.

  • Maternity & Obstetric Services

    Prenatal and postnatal care, normal and high-risk pregnancy management, painless delivery options, and cesarean sections when necessary.

  • Minimally Invasive Surgeries

    Advanced laparoscopic and hysteroscopic procedures for conditions like fibroids, endometriosis, and ovarian cysts with minimal recovery time.

  • Fertility & Reproductive Care

    Comprehensive fertility assessments, ovulation induction, intrauterine insemination (IUI), and specialized treatments for recurrent miscarriages.

  • Menopause Management

    Personalized care for managing menopausal symptoms and maintaining health during this significant life transition.

  • Urogynecology Services

    Specialized care for pelvic floor disorders, urinary incontinence, and other urogynecological conditions affecting women.
